This course will use the `official UVic standard grading scale <https://www.uvic.ca/calendar/future/undergrad/index.php#/policy/S1AAgoGuV?bc=true&bcCurrent=14%20-%20Grading&bcGroup=Undergraduate%20Academic%20Regulations&bcItemType=policies>`_ Your final grade will be determined by your scores on in-class exams and weekly laboratory assignments. Lab assignments *will require additional time* beyond the scheduled three hour lab period. These assignments are due before the next lab meeting (1 week to complete). There is no final exam.
